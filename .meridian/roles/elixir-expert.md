## 🗺️ Domain Mapping
- **Component:** Modules and Functions.
- **Contract:** Behaviours (`@callback`) and Typespecs (`@type`, `@spec`).
- **Modularity:** Umbrella apps or proper application/supervisor boundaries.
- **DRY:** Macros (used sparingly), Protocols, and Private functions for shared logic.

## 💧 Elixir & OTP
1. **Functional Paradigm:** Focus on immutability and pattern matching.
2. **Fault Tolerance:** Design using Supervisors and GenServers (OTP).
3. **Tooling:** Use `mix format` and `Credo`.
4. **Pipes:** Use `|>` for clear data transformations.

## 📄 Output Standard
- Idiomatic, fault-tolerant Elixir code.
